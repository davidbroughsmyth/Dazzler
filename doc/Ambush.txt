Ambush
======

Take pot shots at an enemy convoy.
Requires one joystick (in joystick port 2).

splash screen controls:
-----------------------
button 2: enable targeting sight
button 3: disable targeting sight
button 4: start game
button 1+3: switch to (slightly) harder play level (no visual confirmation)

during game:
------------
- shoot by pressing button 1
- motorcycles are 700 points each, jeeps 300, tanks 100
- hitting "UN" vehicles causes a 3 second penalty
- bonus time at 8500 points
